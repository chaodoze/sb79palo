The City Council Agenda Packet is published 11 days before the meeting and uploaded to the City's web pages after 5:30 p.m. on Thursdays. Members of the public may provide public comments on non-agendized items via email or in person, and on agendized items via email, in person, teleconference, or phone. For more information, including how to participate in meetings and access translation services, please visit our Resources for Meeting Attendance Webpage. For meeting videos please visit the City's Youtube Page or Midpen Media Center.
