City Council Summer & Winter Breaks
The City Council annually approves a Summer & Winter Break an adopted Resolution. Within those dates, Council does not hold regular Council or Standing Committee meetings. At the December 17, 2025 City Council meeting, City Council approved Resolution 10262 to adopt the 2026 City Council and Committee Calendar. The Summer Break is June 19 - July 31, 2026, and the Winter Break is December 22, 2026 - January 1, 2027.
Meeting Schedule
The regular schedule for Council meetings are as follows:

    City Council Meetings are held on the first three Mondays of the month at 5:30 p.m.
    City/School Liaison Committee meets monthly on the third Thursday at 7:00 p.m.
    Council Appointed Officers Committee meets as needed.
    Finance Committee meets monthly on the first and third Tuesday at 5:30 p.m.
    Policy and Services Committee meets monthly on the second Tuesday at 6:00 p.m.
    Rail Committee meets every other month on the third Tuesday at 2:30 p.m.
    Retail Committee meets every other month on third Wednesday at 9:00 a.m.
