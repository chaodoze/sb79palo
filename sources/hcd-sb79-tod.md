Senate Bill (SB) 79 makes qualifying “transit-oriented housing developments” an allowed use on sites zoned for residential, mixed, or commercial development that are located near specified transit stops in urban transit counties. The law takes effect on July 1, 2026.SB 79, signed by Governor Newsom on October 10, 2025, is codified in Government Code sections 65912.155–65912.162. The California Department of Housing and Community Development (HCD) oversees compliance by reviewing local SB 79 ordinances and TOD alternative plans for substantial compliance. HCD is also required to promulgate standards for inclusion of SB 79 sites in the housing element sites inventory.
